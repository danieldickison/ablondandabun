Travel

Flying

We recommend flying into Cincinnati Northern Kentucky International Airport (CVG), which, confusingly, is in Kentucky. From CVG, you can rent a car or take Lyft/Uber.

In Cincinnati

There is a free streetcar that runs from Arnoff Center Station near the Kinley Hotel to Rhinegeist Brewery.

Alternatively, Lyft and Uber are available and is a short ride to the wedding venue. There will be valet parking at the brewery for those who prefer to drive.

Lodging

Kinley Cincinnati Downtown
The link will take you to the Downey-Dickison Wedding Block booking page.

There are plenty of additional hotels in Northern Kentucky and downtown Cincinnati.

If you are looking for a neighborhood experience, we recommend renting an AirBnB in the “Over-The-Rhine” neighborhood, which is a historic former working-class German neighborhood, now home to gastropubs, cafes, shopping and of course — Rhinegeist Brewery!

FAQ

Is there a possibility of postponement due to COVID?

Our original date was October 9, 2021. Due to the delta strain of COVID-19, we decided to postpone to August 12, 2022.

What are the COVID restrictions and safety plans?

In order to protect our family, friends, and venue staff, we’re asking all guests to be fully vaccinated or to have had a negative COVID test result within 48 hours of the event. Because this is an indoor event, all guests may be required to wear masks except while eating and drinking.

Make sure to enter your email address when you RSVP so we can send you any last minute updates, if necessary.

What should I wear?

Cocktail attire.

Can I bring my kids?
We LOVE your kids! But due to size and nature of the venue, this will be an adult-only affair.
Do I need an ID to drink?

Sure do, unless you’re over the age of 80.

Do I need to RSVP for Thursday Drinks and Saturday Pastries?

No, come if you can. Please just RSVP for the Friday ceremony & reception.
